Which visual style fits the superadmin portal?

This is an internal ops tool — pick the aesthetic that feels right for the people running it.

A

Dark Nav + Light Content

Dark sidebar, clean white workspace. The industry standard for ops dashboards. High contrast, comfortable for long sessions.

B

Full Dark Mode

Everything dark. Feels like a control room — serious, focused, zero distraction. Good for monitoring-heavy workflows. Harder to read tables at a glance.

C

Full Light — Clean SaaS

All white, light grey nav. Bright and airy — easy to scan tables and data. Feels more like a product than a console. Can feel too "soft" for an admin tool.
