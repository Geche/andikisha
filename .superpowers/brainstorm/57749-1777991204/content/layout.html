Which navigation layout for the superadmin portal?

Pick the shell that fits how a superadmin works — heavy on tenant management, quick access to alerts and health.

A
Compact Icon Sidebar

Compact — Max content space

Icon-only sidebar, hover to see labels. Best for power users who know where everything is. Dense and efficient.

B
Expanded Sidebar

Expanded — Always-visible labels

Full-width sidebar with section labels always visible. Easier to navigate, good for a team using the portal occasionally.

C
Sidebar + Detail Panel

3-Panel — List + Detail side by side

Left nav, center tenant list, right detail panel. Click a tenant to see their info without leaving the list. Great for support workflows.
