What goes in the nav?

Phase 1 has 7 domains. Three ways to group them into a sidebar. Onboarding is folded into Tenants in all options.

A

Flat — 6 top-level items

Every domain is a direct nav item. Simple, no grouping, no sub-menus.

Nav items: Dashboard · Tenants · Plans & Licences · Feature Flags · Audit Log · Platform Config

B

Grouped — section headers recommended

Items grouped under quiet section labels. Scales gracefully when Phase 2 items are added.

CUSTOMERS
PLATFORM

Customers: Tenants · Plans & Licences · Feature Flags
Platform: Audit Log · Platform Config
Top: Dashboard  |  Bottom: Settings (auth/sessions)

C

Grouped — entity-first

Groups by what you manage, not by function. Plans & Flags live under Tenants conceptually.

TENANTS
SYSTEM

Tenants: All Tenants · Plans & Licences · Feature Flags
System: Audit Log · Platform Config
Top: Dashboard  |  Bottom: Settings

In all options: Auth & Sessions lives in a Settings item pinned to the bottom of the sidebar — it's not a daily-use section, no need to pollute the main nav.
